Spotify Banner Size for Artists Image in 2024: Everything You Need to Know

As an artist, your Spotify profile is your digital storefront, and the banner image is your prime real estate. It's the first thing visitors see, and it plays a crucial role in making a lasting impression. But with potential changes in 2024, it's important to stay updated on the right size to ensure your image displays perfectly.

Ensure that the images you upload to your artist profile adhere to these criteria:

  • Only upload images in .jpeg, .png, or .gif formats.

  • Avatars must have a minimum dimension of 750px x 750px.

  • Header images should be at least 2660px x 1140px.

  • For your image gallery, each image should be at least 690px x 500px.

  • The maximum file size for any image is 20MB.

  • Refrain from using images that contain infringing, offensive content, or busy backgrounds.

  • Avoid incorporating text, advertising, or promotional content such as tour or album announcements.

  • Adhere to our copyright policy in your image selections.

  • We reserve the right to remove images that do not comply with our image guidelines, copyright policy, terms and conditions, or the Spotify Platform Rules.

Further information on acceptable and prohibited content:

  • Intellectual property protection: Do not upload images that infringe on copyrights, trademarks, or personal image rights, such as:

    • Copyright: Most countries have laws to protect original works (e.g., books, music, art). Typically, these laws do not cover ideas or facts but may protect unique expressions of ideas. For specific information, refer to the WIPO’s Global Directory.

    • Trademark: Trademarks (like words, slogans, symbols, or designs) distinguish one company's products or services from another's. Trademark laws aim to prevent consumer confusion about the source of a service or product.

    • Personal images: Personality rights prevent unauthorized public or commercial use of an individual's name, image, or likeness. Consent is required to post someone's image.
